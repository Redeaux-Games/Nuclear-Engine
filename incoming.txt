RED AI - RedAI is an AI system the helps create the 
next level of game play by creating sophisticated 
NPC AI using RedAI, speech to text, text to speech, 
machine learning, custom reponses, facial expressions 
and speech synthesis. 

The developer using the system will have complete 
control over the actions of the NPC and it's responses.

A player can have a full conversation with an NPC 
and the NPC will respond with voice, facial 
expressions, hand movements and more.

These settings can also be changed depending of the NPC.

So for example, 

A woman will have a female speech synthesis and men 
will male speech synthesis. 

Kids will have appropriate voice, monsters, aliens, etc.

The developer will also have full control or allow it 
to be automated.

Although this is just a simple example of how this system can be used.

This will create a mini service or daemon running in the 
background. Required software to make this possible.
